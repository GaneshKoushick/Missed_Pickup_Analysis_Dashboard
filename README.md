## 📊 Insights
- Vendor A shows the **highest missed pickup percentage**, indicating operational inefficiency.  
- Vendor C consistently performs well, making it the **most reliable transporter**.  
- Missed pickup trends fluctuate between **12%–22%**, suggesting seasonal or operational variability.  
- Cost impact of missed pickups is significant, emphasizing the need for vendor reallocation and monitoring.

---

## 🎯 Purpose
This dashboard helps logistics and operations teams:
- Identify underperforming vendors.
- Optimize allocation percentages.
- Reduce missed pickups and associated costs.
- Improve overall supply chain reliability.
